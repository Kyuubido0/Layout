About ZUBIA

Two long-time friends formed Zubia after their daughters were diagnosed with different life-threatening conditions. They were seeking access to the most current information on those conditions so they created a user-friendly app using live-streaming interactive video technology. Today our members are from all areas of health and wellness, including patients and their families, hospitals, charities and foundations, pharmaceutical companies, medical professionals, diet and nutrition experts, scientists, researchers, support groups and more. Together we share our knowledge and experiences to benefit the entire community.
